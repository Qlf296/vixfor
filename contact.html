Frequently asked questions

Is ForgCV really free?

Yes, 100% free. No subscription, no credit card required. All main features — CV, cover letter, PDF export, 8 languages, 6 templates — are completely free and will remain so.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. Your data never leaves your device. We use your browser's localStorage for auto-save. We do not collect any personal information.

Is the PDF compatible with ATS software?

Yes. Our Classic, Minimal and Executive templates are specially optimized for applicant tracking systems (ATS) used by most large companies.

Can I use ForgCV on mobile?

Yes! ForgCV is fully responsive and optimized for all devices — mobile, tablet and desktop.

How many CVs can I create?

As many as you like, with no limits. Create a different CV for each application, adapt your template by industry, and download as many PDFs as you want.
