Tips for a convincing letter

Les recruteurs lisent en moyenne 30 secondes. Faites compter chaque mot.

✏️

250–400 words maximum

One page, no more. Recruiters appreciate conciseness. Every sentence must earn its place.

🎯

Personalise every letter

Mention the company name, the exact role and a concrete reason for your interest. Generic letters get rejected.

📊

Concrete results

Cite numbers: "increased sales by 30%", "managed a team of 5". Evidence beats promises every time.
