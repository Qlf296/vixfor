Keys to a Successful Internship CV

1

Target your CV by sector

Tailor each CV to the company and sector. A CV for a finance internship looks nothing like one for a communications internship.

2

Compelling professional summary

2–3 lines that summarise who you are, your academic background, and what you bring to the table. It is the first thing the recruiter reads.

3

Education first

For an internship, your school, major, and graduation year should come first. Mention courses relevant to the role.

4

Precise technical skills

List the software, languages, tools, and certifications with your actual level. Be honest — you will be tested in the interview.

🎯 Important: For an internship, the cover letter is often more important than the CV itself. It explains your motivation and what you will bring to the role.

What Recruiters Look for in an Internship

🎯

Clear motivation

Why THIS company and THIS role? The recruiter wants to see that you have done your research and understand their needs.

📚

Education/internship fit

Your academic background should align with the internship you are applying for. If not, explain the connection in your cover letter.

⚡

Learning ability

Show that you are curious, self-sufficient, and able to learn fast. Give concrete examples of new skills you have acquired.

Frequently asked questions

When should you send your internship CV?

For a summer internship, send your CV 2 to 3 months in advance. For other periods, 1 month ahead is usually sufficient.

How to find an internship quickly?

Use LinkedIn, Indeed, HelloWork and your school's job boards. Spontaneous applications with a strong CV and a targeted cover letter are often more effective than applying to job postings.

Should an internship CV be one or two pages?

One page maximum. An internship CV must be concise. If you have a lot of experience, select only the most relevant.

Should you mention the desired internship duration on your CV?

No, not on the CV. Mention it in your cover letter or in the subject line of your application email.
