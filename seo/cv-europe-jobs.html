Tips for applying internationally

  • 📄
    Create 2 versions of your CVOne version in French (or your native language) and one in English. Adapt both to the conventions of the target country.
  • 🔗
    LinkedIn in EnglishYour LinkedIn profile should be in English to be visible to international recruiters. Add "Open to work" and specify the target countries.
  • 🌐
    Job sites by countryIndeed (international), EURES (official EU), Glassdoor, LinkedIn Jobs. In NL: Nationale Vacaturebank. In DE: Stepstone, Xing. In FR: France Travail (ex Pôle Emploi), Welcome to the Jungle.
  • 📋
    Europass format for certain countriesThe Europass format is recognised across the entire EU and particularly valued in Germany, Belgium and the European public sector.
