CV format rules in 2026

📏

Length

1 page: 0–5 years of experience
2 pages: 5+ years of experience
Never 3 pages except for academic CVs

🔤

Typography

Sans-serif font (Inter, Calibri, Arial). Body: 10–11pt. Headings: 12–14pt. Margins: 1.5–2cm. Line spacing: 1.15–1.3.

🎨

Colours

Black or dark grey text. 1 accent colour (blue, green, purple). White or very lightly tinted background. Avoid dark backgrounds.

📁

File format

PDF first and foremost. Always named "FirstName_LastName_CV.pdf". Never Word (.docx) unless explicitly requested.

🤖

ATS compatibility

No tables. No multi-column layouts if possible. No background images. Selectable text (not scanned).

📋

Sections in 2026

Summary → Experience → Skills → Education. In 2026, the professional summary is essential (2–3 lines).
