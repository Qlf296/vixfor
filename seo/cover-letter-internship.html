Frequently asked questions

How do I stand out for a highly competitive internship?

Send an unsolicited application rather than waiting for listings, personalise your letter as much as possible, and follow up by phone 5 days after sending.

When should I send my internship cover letter?

2 to 3 months before your desired start date for large corporations, 1 month for SMEs and startups. Summer internships fill up from February–March onwards.

Should I mention my availability in the letter?

Yes, state your preferred period (e.g. "from June 2026 for a duration of 6 months") in your cover letter.

How should I close an internship cover letter?

Proactively propose an interview: "I would be delighted to meet with you to discuss my application in more detail." End with a professional closing.
