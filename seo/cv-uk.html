The UK job market in 2026

The British job market is one of the most competitive in Europe. UK recruiters receive an average of 250 applications per role and spend only 7 seconds on each CV. Your CV must be impeccable at first glance.

6 essential rules for the perfect UK CV

01

No photo

Unlike France, including a photo on a British CV is considered inappropriate and could even expose the employer to discrimination claims.

02

No sensitive personal details

Age, marital status, nationality, religion: never mention them. UK employers are very strict on equal opportunities.

03

2 pages maximum

The standard UK format is 2 A4 pages. Never exceed 2 pages, even with 20 years of experience. 1 page for junior profiles.

04

Personal Statement at the top

Always start with a 'Personal Statement' of 3–4 lines summarising who you are and what you bring. This is the first text read.

05

Action verbs + numbers

Each experience must start with a strong verb (Managed, Led, Delivered, Grew) and include quantified results.

06

ATS format is essential

85% of large UK companies use ATS. Use standard fonts, avoid complex columns and tables.
