Our mission

Every year, millions of people send CVs that do not reflect their true value. Presentation, format, content — these are all barriers standing between talent and opportunity.

ForgCV was designed to eliminate these barriers. We want a student finishing their degree, a career changer, or an expat looking for work in a new country to all be able to create a CV as professional as one prepared by a top recruitment agency.

Free. Simple. Effective. For everyone.
